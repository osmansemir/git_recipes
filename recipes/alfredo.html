Chicken Alfredo

Description

Chicken Alfredo is a creamy, rich pasta dish that's perfect for a comforting dinner. This version combines tender chicken breast with a luxurious Alfredo sauce made from butter, cream, and Parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • Fettuccine
  • Chicken Breast
  • Butter
  • Heavy Cream
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Cook the fettuccine
  2. Season and cook the chicken
  3. Make the Alfredo sauce
  4. Combine pasta and sauce
  5. Add the chicken and garnish with parsley
