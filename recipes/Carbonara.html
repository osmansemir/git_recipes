Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

This classic Spaghetti Carbonara recipe is beloved for its creamy texture and rich flavors. The perfect combination of eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper, this dish is a staple in Italian cuisine and a favorite among pasta lovers worldwide.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Pancetta
  • Eggs
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Pecorino Cheese
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti
  2. Fry the pancetta
  3. Mix eggs and cheeses
  4. Combine pasta with pancetta
  5. Mix pasta with egg mixture off the heat
